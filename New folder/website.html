BUGEMA UNIVERSITY VOLLEYBALL CLUB

BUGEMA UNIVERSITY VOLLEYBALL CLUB WELCOMES YOU!

Bugema university volleyball club is a competitive club that was founded with in the university with the intension of further promotion of sports by the university in various flieds in higher learning. The club comprises of two distinctive genders namely, men well known as the "BU lighters" and ladies as the "She lighters" Bugema University volleyball club plays the serie B category in the UGANDA VOLLEYBALL FEDERATION(UVF)
